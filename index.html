  1. Bid Day

    If you receive a bid, a group of PSE members will deliver it to your dorm, house, etc. Once all of the bids are handed out, there will be a casual event where you can meet active members and the other new members in your class. If you do not receive a bid from PSE, you will hear from our Director of Recruitment with the next steps. You are always welcome to connect with him/her for feedback or questions.

  2. New Member Client Project

    The New Member Project is an immersive experience designed to introduce new members to the core values and real-world applications of Pi Sigma Epsilon. Each year, the project varies in scope, but the structure remains the same—new members are placed in teams of 4-5, led by an experienced member acting as a mentor. Together, they conduct research and develop strategic solutions for a real client, with past partnerships including BIBIBOP Asian Grill, LionsGate, and Procter & Gamble. The project is structured to be manageable alongside coursework, with flexibility to accommodate students’ schedules. Support is always available through team leads, new member educators, and older chapter members, ensuring a rewarding and educational experience.

  3. New Member Professional Development series

    Within 5 weeks, new members will attend weekly New Member Education meetings and workshops. You also meet weekly with your project team members and leaders to work on the client project together, while engaging in other Chapter events

  4. Client Presentation Day

    Client Presentation Day is the exciting culmination of the New Member Project, where teams showcase their hard work and strategic solutions to the actual client. In front of the entire chapter, new members deliver their final pitches, demonstrating their research, creativity, and problem-solving skills. This event not only provides valuable presentation experience but also allows teams to receive direct feedback from industry professionals. At the end of the presentations, the winning team is announced, recognizing their outstanding effort and innovative approach. It’s a high-energy and rewarding experience that highlights the talent and dedication of PSE’s newest members.

  5. Initiation

    nitiation is a formal and meaningful ceremony where new members officially become part of Pi Sigma Epsilon. It marks the culmination of their journey through the new member process, celebrating their dedication and growth. The event is attended by the entire chapter, creating a welcoming and supportive atmosphere. New members recite the PSE creed, receive their official membership certificates, and are recognized for their hard work.
